Our Policies

Please review and follow the guidelines below.

Booking Policy

- Appointments must be booked in advance.

- A non-refundable deposit is required to secure an appointment.

- Deposit go towards your balance on the day of services.

Lateness Policy

- Clients must come on time.

- Any client who is over 15 minutes late may have their appointment canceled or rescheduled, and the deposit forfeited.

Cancellation and Rescheduling

- Cancellation and rescheduling must be made at least 24 hours in advance.

- If you cancel less than 24 hours in advance, or don't show up, your deposit will be lost.

Payment

- The remaining balance after the deposit is due on the day of services.

- We accept cash or Momo.

- We accept foreign currency.

Hair Preparation

- Please come with your hair clean, disentangled, and product-free.

- An extra charge come on bleaching, cornrows, and washing of lace.

Content Policy

- We take pictures or videos of your hair for social media promotion.

- If you prefer not to be recorded or photographed, Please let us know before your appointment.

Service Policy

- No extra guests unless approved in advance.

- Please confirm your style, length, and color before your appointment.

- Once a service is completed completed, no refunds.

- We value respect and professionalism.

- Any form of disrespect and unprofessionalism may result in a refusal of services.

Business Hours

Monday - Saturday 8:00 AM - 10:00 PM
Sunday 10:00 AM - 8:00 PM

Strictly by appointments to ensure availability.

Appointments outside the above hours incure extra charges.
